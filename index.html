Connect With Me

My name is Murad Alkaylanie. I was born and raised in Iraq. After getting my diploma in high school, I traveled to a lot of different countries. Finding my path was a difficult road, but gratefully I am happy to where I stand today. I have learned and appreciated the way my life has been planned out. My family and friends always tell me that I am a hardworking person and very independent. Being a responsible personality makes me feel strong enough to do whatever I want whenever I feel like it.

In my spare time, I like to read and play video games. Traveling is my favorite part to explore and a chance to meet new people and see the various beautiful places this world holds. I am always eager to learn new things that will help me be a better person.

This world is a great place to be in, you only need to find your true self and be who you meant to be, a great-good one!
